# -SQL_Music_Store_Analysis
This project analyzes an online music store database using SQL to extract insights related to sales, customers, and music trends.  The analysis focuses on solving real-world business problems such as identifying top customers, best-selling tracks, and revenue distribution across different regions.

🛠 Tech Stack
PostgreSQL
SQL
PgAdmin4
📂 Dataset

The dataset represents a digital music store and includes relational tables such as:

Customers
Invoices
Invoice_Items
Tracks
Albums
Artists
Genres

🔍 Analysis Performed

Customer spending analysis
Revenue by country and city
Top-selling tracks and artists
Most popular music genres
Sales distribution across different regions
📊 Key Insights
A small group of customers contributes a significant portion of total revenue
Certain genres dominate overall sales
Revenue is concentrated in specific countries and cities
Top artists consistently drive a large share of purchases
